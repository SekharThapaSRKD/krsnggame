# Krishna Story Mode

A real story-mode browser game built with plain HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

## What It Is

The old card-heavy chapter flow has been replaced with a playable story mode:

- Act I: storm crossing to Gokul
- Act II: butter-stealth courtyard
- Act III: Kaliya boss fight
- Act IV: Govardhan rescue mission
- Act V: Mathura arena battle
- Act VI: Dwaraka finale and last arc

## Story Coverage

The side timeline groups the full Krishna leela list into six story acts:

- Early Pastimes
- Boyhood Pastimes in Vrindavan
- Rasa Leela
- Mathura Leela
- Dwaraka and royal pastimes
- Final pastimes

## Run It

Open [index.html](/Users/sekharthapa/Documents/New project/index.html) in a browser.

## Controls

- `Arrow keys` or `WASD` to move
- `Space`, `Enter`, `F`, or `E` to interact, attack, recruit, or continue dialogue
- On mobile, use the on-screen buttons
